Frequently Asked Questions

What makes FlowHabits different?

FlowHabits provides active guidance instead of passive tracking. Rather than just reminding you to do something, we guide you through each step with helpful tips and contextual advice.

What happens if I miss a day?

No worries! FlowHabits uses a momentum system instead of streaks, so missing a day won't reset your progress. Just continue when you're ready.

Is FlowHabits free?

FlowHabits offers a free tier with one complete flow and basic analytics. Premium features include all flows, advanced analytics, and customization options.

How does data sync work?

Your data syncs automatically across all your devices when signed in with your Apple ID using CloudKit.

Can I customize my flows?

Premium users can customize flows and create their own. Free users have access to our carefully crafted default flows.
